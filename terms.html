Dieu Khoan Su Dung

Cap nhat lan cuoi: 14/03/2026

Chao mung ban den voi Horos. Bang viec tai xuong va su dung ung dung Horos ("Ung dung"), ban dong y tuan thu cac dieu khoan su dung sau day ("Dieu khoan"). Vui long doc ky truoc khi su dung.

1. Chap Nhan Dieu Khoan

Bang viec truy cap hoac su dung Ung dung, ban xac nhan rang ban da doc, hieu va dong y bi rang buoc boi cac Dieu khoan nay. Neu ban khong dong y, vui long khong su dung Ung dung.

2. Mo Ta Dich Vu

Horos la ung dung tu vi Viet Nam cung cap cac tinh nang bao gom:

  • Tao va xem la so tu vi ca nhan
  • Phan tich boi tinh duyen
  • Rut que Cosmic Fortune
  • Tao chan dung phu the bang AI
  • Chat voi AI ve tu vi
  • Xem van the hang thang
  • Phan tich ngu hanh ban menh

3. Mien Tru Trach Nhiem

Noi dung tu vi chi mang tinh chat tham khao va giai tri. Horos khong dam bao tinh chinh xac, day du hoac dang tin cay cua bat ky noi dung tu vi, boi toan hoac du doan nao duoc cung cap trong Ung dung. Ban khong nen dua ra cac quyet dinh quan trong trong cuoc song (tai chinh, suc khoe, phap ly) chi dua tren noi dung cua Ung dung.

4. Dang Ky & Mua Hang Trong Ung Dung

Horos Premium cung cap cac tinh nang cao cap thong qua goi dang ky:

  • Thanh toan duoc xu ly thong qua Apple App Store
  • Goi dang ky tu dong gia han tru khi ban huy truoc khi ket thuc ky hien tai
  • Ban co the huy dang ky bat ky luc nao thong qua Cai dat > Apple ID > Dang ky tren thiet bi cua ban
  • Khong hoan tien cho phan thoi gian chua su dung cua ky dang ky hien tai
  • Ban dung thu mien phi (neu co) se ket thuc khi ban dang ky goi tra phi

5. Tai Khoan & Quyen Su Dung

  • Ban phai tu 13 tuoi tro len de su dung Ung dung
  • Ban chiu trach nhiem ve viec su dung Ung dung cua minh
  • Ban khong duoc su dung Ung dung cho bat ky muc dich bat hop phap nao
  • Ban khong duoc sao chep, sua doi hoac phan phoi lai Ung dung

6. Noi Dung AI

Ung dung su dung cong nghe AI (Google Gemini) de tao noi dung. Noi dung do AI tao ra:

  • Chi mang tinh chat tham khao va giai tri
  • Co the khong chinh xac hoac day du
  • Khong thay the loi khuyen chuyen mon (y te, phap ly, tai chinh)
  • Duoc tao tu dong va co the thay doi theo thoi gian

7. So Huu Tri Tue

Tat ca noi dung, thiet ke, bieu tuong, do hoa va phan mem trong Ung dung la tai san cua Horos va duoc bao ve boi luat so huu tri tue. Ban khong duoc sao chep, sua doi hoac phan phoi bat ky phan nao cua Ung dung ma khong co su dong y bang van ban cua chung toi.

8. Gioi Han Trach Nhiem

Trong pham vi phap luat cho phep, Horos khong chiu trach nhiem ve bat ky thiet hai truc tiep, gian tiep, ngau nhien, dac biet hoac hau qua nao phat sinh tu viec su dung hoac khong the su dung Ung dung.

9. Thay Doi Dieu Khoan

Chung toi co the cap nhat cac Dieu khoan nay theo thoi gian. Viec ban tiep tuc su dung Ung dung sau khi thay doi co nghia la ban chap nhan cac Dieu khoan moi.

10. Luat Ap Dung

Cac Dieu khoan nay duoc dieu chinh boi phap luat Viet Nam. Bat ky tranh chap nao phat sinh se duoc giai quyet tai toa an co tham quyen tai Viet Nam.
